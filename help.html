Help Page

How to Use the Perfume Shop Website

  • Home: Welcome page with a brief introduction to the perfume shop and navigation links.
  • Gallery: Browse our collection of perfumes. Click on any perfume image to see more details and information about the fragrance.
  • Booking: Use this page to reserve or order your favorite perfume. Fill in your name, contact details, and select the perfume you wish to buy.
  • Help: You're here! Find instructions for browsing, searching, and purchasing perfumes.
  • About: Learn more about our perfume shop, our mission, and how to contact us.

Instructions

  • Use the navigation bar at the top to move between Home, Gallery, Booking, Help, and About pages.
  • On the Gallery page, click on a perfume image to view its details, including scent notes and price.
  • On the Booking page, complete all required fields to place your order or reservation. Double-check your contact information before submitting.
  • If you have questions or need assistance, visit this Help page or contact us through the About page.
